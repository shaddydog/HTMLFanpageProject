Short introduction on who our Lord and Savior Blitzcrank is

Blitzcrank, also known as The Great Steam Golumn, is a playable champion in the League of Legends PC video game.He is a tank who is primarily played in the support role due to his ability kit. He is known for his rocket launched hand that acts like a hook, once it hits an enemy champion or unit it will bring them back to Blitzcrak's position.

Blitzcrank has a high skill level ceiling but is also a fun champion to pick up as a new support player. He is also a high risk high reward champion, in that if you can hook the right target you may end up getting an easy kill for your team; however, if you miss that hook, the other team may collapse on you. Blitzcrank's Rocket Grab is the main ability in his kit, if he misses it or hooks the wrong champion, it could prove to be a fatal mistake for the great steam golumn and his team.
